DREAMZONE® is dedicated to improve your sleep with individual solutions within mattresses and beds. Quality and functionality are essential and have been so since the establishment in Denmark in 2003. DREAMZONE is developed in Scandinavia and provides great value for money. You can be sure to find the ideal solution from the wide variety of designs and materials. DREAMZONE offers spring and foam mattresses that fit into a bed frame of your choice, box mattresses for a more simple and light look, continental beds that combine functionality with design, as well as adjustable beds that provide versatility and flexibility. For even more comfort and to protect your mattress, DREAMZONE also offers a variety of top mattresses and mattress protectors. The majority of the mattresses are certified according to OEKO-® Standard 100, which is the world’s leading health label for textiles. Furthermore, DREAMZONE comes with a guarantee of up to 25 years.
